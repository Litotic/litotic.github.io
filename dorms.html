Dorms

A proud boarding school tradition, each housing dorm upholds a different, distinct history and boasts of a unique, characteristic reputation.
Upon admittance and orientation, fresh off the black carriages, students are evaluated by the Dark Mirror, the famous fortune-divining artifact.
After being assigned to the dorm supposedly meant to draw out your soul's greatest potential (although, as as modern educational institution,
transfers are indeed possible), students continue the rest of orientation in the care of their Dorm Head and Vice Dorm Head and other senior counsel.

From students to students, a rich and treasured community is built and passed down.

Now... will you be the next to carry the torch?
