Year Advancement, Graduation

First through third years enjoy the finest quality arcane education on NRC's founding Sage Isle campus.
Third years will spent their spring semester preparing to embark on study abroad internships or domestic apprenticeships in their fourth year fall.
In fourth year spring, the pre-graduates are back on campus to present their final projects, reflect on their learning, and pass down mentorship to underclassmen.
After graduation, NRC is proud to boast a strong and steadfast network of alumni supporting alumni, and often invites them back to its hallowed halls
as guest lecturers or recruiters.

Visitors and Mailing

Although NRC is typically closed to outside visitors in order to respect the focus of its students' education,
exceptions are often made for alumni with educational assistance purposes. For family and friends, please limit yourselves to sending letters and packages
to express your feelings outside of family emergencies or designated opening days, such as the Halloween Festival or the Spring Fair.
