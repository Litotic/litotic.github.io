Clubs

Of course, we understand the importance of balancing studies with all other aspects of a well-rounded education and the development of youth.
This is especially true as mages in training, whose emotional states can adversely affect spellcasting in an unpredictable and inadvisable way.
We encourage all students to regularly attend opportunities for outside reflection, and mandate every student join at least one extracurricular club.
Here is a brief glimpse of the myriad student clubs that have been formed with faculty advising and school funding.
